Who are we?

inPixel Studios is a game design studio creating content within
and for the Minecraft Marketplace. Our focus is to give you the
content and experience that you deserve. We can do this with
creators from all around the globe from 3D modellers,
Level designers, programmers and Texture designers.

What is our goal?

Our main goal is to deliver content that will provide players with a
memorable experience through immersion, storytelling, and to
provide endless amounts of fun for families and friends. Everyone at
our studio has a deep compassion for Minecraft, we are motivated to
share our ideas with others who love Minecraft just as we do.

inPixel Studios is an official
Minecraft partner.
